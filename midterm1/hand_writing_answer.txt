1. What are the four steps of How-to-Solve-It? (10%)

    1. Understand the Problem
    2. Find a way to solve and seperate the problem into steps
    3. Practicing your solutions
    4. Look Back

2. What are the three steps of TDD? (10%)

    1. Define expected outputs 
    2. Write the test
    3. Write the corresponding code to pass the test
